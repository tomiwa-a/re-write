Features

Everything you need

Offline-First

Works entirely without internet. Your notes and drawings are saved locally in your browser's database and are always available.

Shareable Links

Generate a read-only link and share your work with friends instantly. They can view your notes and canvases without an account.

Optional Sync

Sign in only when you want to sync across devices. You own your data, and we only store it when you explicitly choose to.

Workflow

How It Works

1

Create

Start a new note for text or a canvas for visual ideas. Use the powerful block editor or the infinite whiteboard.

2

Save

Everything is stored instantly in your browser (IndexedDB). No loading spinners, no network latency.

3

Share

Want to show someone? Generate a link. Want to switch devices? Sign in to sync.

Built for privacy and freedom

reWrite is designed with a simple philosophy: your data belongs to you. Most apps hold your data hostage to a subscription. We don't. Everything works offline by default.
